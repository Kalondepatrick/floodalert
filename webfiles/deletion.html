Data Deletion Instructions for FloodAlert

Requesting Data Deletion

If you wish to request the deletion of your data from FloodAlert, please email the lead developer pkalonde@mlw.mw.

  • Provide Necessary Information: In your email, you will be expected to provide certain information to verify your identity. This may include your Facebook username and specific flood alert that you are asking for deletion.

    • Processing Your Request

    Verification: Our team will review and verify the information provided in your request to accuracy of the data deletion process.

    Processing Time: Please note that the data deletion process may take a reasonable amount of time to complete (Our team is currently so small). We appreciate your patience during this period.

    Important Information

    Retained Data: Certain data may be retained for legal or regulatory reasons, even after your data deletion request has been processed. We will only retain the minimum necessary information required for compliance with applicable laws.

    Thank you for your understanding and cooperation in ensuring the privacy and security of your data with FloodAlert.
